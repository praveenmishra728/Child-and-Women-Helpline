About SurakshaAI

An AI-powered Women & Child Safety Portal — built by the Government of India to protect every woman and child across the nation.

Our Mission

SurakshaAI was created with one mission: to ensure that every woman and child in India has a safe, accessible, and efficient platform to report incidents, seek help, and receive the support they need — anytime, anywhere.

By combining Artificial Intelligence with Government-backed infrastructure, we provide instant safety guidance, intelligent incident analysis, and seamless connectivity to all national helplines.

Privacy & Security

  • Data Encryption: All personal data and reports are AES-256 encrypted at rest and in transit.
  • Anonymous Reporting: You can submit reports without revealing your identity.
  • Passwordless Auth: We use secure OTP-based authentication — no passwords stored.
  • IT Act Compliant: Fully compliant with the Information Technology Act 2000 and DPDP Act 2023.
  • Government Oversight: Operated under the Ministry of Women & Child Development, Government of India.
