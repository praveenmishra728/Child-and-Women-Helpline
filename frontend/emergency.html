Emergency Helplines

These helplines are available 24×7. If you are in immediate danger, call the appropriate number below. All calls are free of charge.

If you are in immediate danger, call 112 (Police) RIGHT NOW.

Primary Emergency Numbers

🚔
112
Police Emergency

General police emergency. Call for any immediate threat to life or safety.

Call 112 Now
👩
181
Women Helpline

National Women Helpline — domestic violence, harassment, and all women's issues.

Call 181 Now
👶
1098
Child Helpline

Childline — for children in abuse, distress, or any child-related emergency.

Call 1098 Now
🚑
108
Ambulance

Medical emergency — injuries, illness, or any life-threatening health situation.

Call 108 Now
💻
1930
Cyber Crime

National Cyber Crime Helpline — online fraud, sextortion, cyberstalking.

Call 1930 Now
